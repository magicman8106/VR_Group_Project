Activity 1

Activity 1 requires the user to make external measurements of the pig, to determine age and gender.

The user will demonstrate proper hygine and saftey precautions, and will learn about external qualities to perform basic pig examination

Step One

Obtain a fetal pig, dissection tray, gloves, ruler, scissors, a probe, and a scalpel.

Step Two

Using a ruler, measure the length. (Determine its approximate age)

Step Three

Make sure to rest the pig on its dorsal side (back).

Step Four

Tie the other ends of the string to the bulbs on the dissecting tray to hold open the pig. (Identify its gender) *Refer button
