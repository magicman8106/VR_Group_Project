Activity 2

Activity 2 requires the user to make internal incisions to identify and examine anatomical parts of the pigs mouth.
Along with the proper saftey precautions, the user will have to make incisions in the mouth of pig, and identify key oral components including the epiglottis.

Step One

Obtain a fetal pig, dissection tray, gloves, ruler, string, scissors, a probe, and a scalpel.

Step Two

Expose the organs of the mouth and pharynx by inserting your scissors in the angle of the mouth bilaterally (on both sides) dissecting caudally
(in the direction of the tail).

Step Three

You can continue your incisions/dissections until you see the epiglottis. *Refer button.

Step Four

Carefully pull the epiglottis, and continue to cut dorsal to it. Continue to cut until you see the larynx with its opening, the glottis.

Step Five

Once you have made your bilateral dissections, you will have to pry open the oral cavity using your hands. The mouth should be easily swung open. *Refer button

Step Six

Wrap the pig in damp paper towels and put the specimen into a plastic bag.
